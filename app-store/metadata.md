## App Name
Voice to Action

## Subtitle
Civic Accountability Platform

## Description
Voice to Action is the world's first civic accountability social media platform. Sign petitions, participate in polls, hold politicians and corporations accountable, and connect with communities that share your values.

### Key Features
- Create and sign petitions that reach decision-makers
- Vote in polls on the issues that matter to you
- Rate and review politicians, businesses, and public figures
- Join communities built around causes you care about
- Get verified to show you're a real person with a real voice
- Follow the issues and people that matter to you
- See real-time trending civic issues in your country and worldwide
